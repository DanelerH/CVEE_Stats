At the start of a campaign, create a character that consists of a race, a race variant, and a class with three abilities, unless stated otherwise.

At this time, you may apply any passive race abilities. i.e. Dwarves gain an additional 1000 gold, Gnomes get four random treasure cards, etc.

Your character starts with D20 + 15 Stamina.
