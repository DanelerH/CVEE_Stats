Treasure Cards

Five treasure cards will be randomly selected by the GM (by rolling 5D21). After one has been purchased, another random treasure card will be added to the shop.

Your character starts off with no items. Before the game begins, you may purchase items and treasure cards for your character to use in the adventure.

All characters start with 1000 gold, unless stated otherwise.
