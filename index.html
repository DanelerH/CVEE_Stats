Treasure Cards

At the start of a campaign, create a character that consists of a race, a race variant, and a class with three abilities, unless stated otherwise.

Your character starts off with no items. Before the game begins, you may purchase items and treasure cards from the shop for your character to use in the adventure.

All cards and abilities can and likely will be changed for balancing issues.

All prior campaigns will follow the rules set out during each respective campaigns.

All rules will be explained in further detail on their concerned pages.
